SWIFTE

Ride Sharing App

Swifte is an iOS carpooling app featuring ride-search, ride-tracking, group-creating, and social profile features. I completed the redesign of the entire user interface based on user research. I wanted to create a better user experience than the design given to me, as well as implement new features that the company couldn't due to their old design. I aimed to provide a simpler yet visually pleasing experience by redesigning navigation workflow and changing color/typography.

MYBEEBLE

Textbook Buying App

MyBeeble is a mobile app that lets you find the best deals in books, whether they come from a library or bookstore. I spearheaded the user interface redesign for both android and iOS, for app store release. I implemented a simple and clean layout with a color scheme chosen for branding purposes that allow users to navigate through the app without graphics obstruction or complication.
